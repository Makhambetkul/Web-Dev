Banana Smoothie

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Banana 1
Milk 1 cup
Honey 1 tablespoon
Ice cupes As desired

Step 1: Blend all ingredients until smooth.

Step 2: Pour into a glass and serve chilled.

Caprese Salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Tomatoes 2
Mozzarella 1 ball
Fresh basil 6-8 leaves
Olive oil 1 tablespoon
Balsamic vinegar 1 tablespoon
Salt To taste
Pepper To taste

Step1: Layer tomato, mozzarella, and basil on a plate.

Step2: Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

Step3: Add salt and pepper to taste.

Avocado Toast

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 1 slice
Avocado 1/2 avocado
Salt To taste
Pepper To taste
Optional topping As desired

Step1: Spread mashed avocado on toasted bread.

Step2: Add salt, pepper, and optional toppings.
